FALL 2012 | APPLAUSE.COM

Applause is an analytics tool that offers a comprehensive data about how users really feel about the app. Applause allows its users to build and curate collections of apps they like or want to watch. We designed a simple and clean interface to house the data visualizations we created to cover multiple versions of the app. The idea is to be able to watch as an application grows and evolves over time as observed by its users.
